## 3. Launch Day Plan

### Timing

- **Posting time:** 12:01 AM PST (PH resets at midnight PST)
- **Peak engagement windows:** 6-9 AM PST, 12-2 PM PST, 5-7 PM PST

### Launch Day Schedule

| Time (PST) | Action | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 12:01 AM | Product goes live on PH | [Hunter/Maker] |
| 12:05 AM | Post maker's first comment | [Maker] |
| 6:00 AM | Send launch email to subscriber list | [Name] |
| 6:00 AM | Post to Twitter/X, LinkedIn, social | [Name] |
| 6:00 AM | Notify supporter group (Slack/Discord/DM) | [Name] |
| 7:00 AM | Share in relevant communities (HN, Reddit, Discord) | [Name] |
| 8:00 AM | First metrics check | [Name] |
| 9:00 AM | Send reminder to supporters who haven't engaged | [Name] |
| 10:00 AM | Respond to ALL comments on PH | [Maker] |
| 12:00 PM | Second wave social posts | [Name] |
| 12:00 PM | Check ranking position | [Name] |
| 2:00 PM | Respond to new comments, engage with feedback | [Maker] |
| 4:00 PM | Third wave social push if ranking well | [Name] |
| 6:00 PM | Evening comment response sweep | [Maker] |
| 11:00 PM | End-of-day metrics and ranking check | [Name] |

### Comment Response Strategy

- **Respond to EVERY comment** — within 30 minutes during waking hours
- **Positive comments:** Thank genuinely, add detail, ask follow-up question
- **Feature requests:** Acknowledge, add to roadmap if valid, be honest about timeline
- **Questions:** Answer thoroughly, link to docs/demos if relevant
- **Critical feedback:** Thank them, address the concern honestly, show you're listening

### Important Rules

- NEVER ask people to upvote — ask them to "check it out on Product Hunt"
- PH penalizes coordinated upvoting from same IP/location
- Genuine comments are more valuable than upvotes
- Don't send PH direct links in mass emails (use your landing page with a PH badge)
