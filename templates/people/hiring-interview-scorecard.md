### BIAS CHECK

Before submitting, review your evaluation for potential bias:

- [ ] Am I evaluating evidence of skills rather than pattern-matching on background?
- [ ] Would I rate this the same if the candidate had a different background?
- [ ] Am I anchoring on a first impression rather than the full interview?
- [ ] Am I penalizing communication style differences rather than actual communication ability?
- [ ] Am I holding this candidate to the same standard as others?
