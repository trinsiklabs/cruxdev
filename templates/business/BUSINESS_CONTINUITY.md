## 7. Recovery & Restoration

### Short-Term (1-7 Days)

- [ ] Restore critical business functions per priority
- [ ] Implement workarounds for non-critical functions
- [ ] Daily status updates to all stakeholders

### Medium-Term (1-4 Weeks)

- [ ] Restore all business functions
- [ ] Address backlog created during disruption
- [ ] Begin root cause analysis

### Long-Term (1-3 Months)

- [ ] Complete root cause analysis
- [ ] Implement permanent fixes
- [ ] Update this plan based on lessons learned
- [ ] Conduct post-incident review
