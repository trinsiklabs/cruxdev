## OPERATING AGREEMENT OF [COMPANY NAME] LLC

This Operating Agreement ("Agreement") of [COMPANY NAME] LLC (the "Company") is entered into as of [DATE] by the Members identified below.

---

### Article I: Formation

1.1 **Name:** [COMPANY NAME] LLC

1.2 **Formation:** The Company was formed on [DATE] by filing Articles of Organization with the [STATE] Secretary of State.

1.3 **Registered Agent:** [Name and address of registered agent]

1.4 **Principal Office:** [Address]

1.5 **Purpose:** [Describe the business purpose — or "any lawful business activity"]

1.6 **Term:** The Company shall continue in perpetuity unless dissolved in accordance with this Agreement.

---

### Article II: Members & Capital

2.1 **Members:**

| Member | Capital Contribution | Membership Interest (%) | Voting Rights |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Name] | $[Amount] / [Description of non-cash] | [X]% | [X]% |
| [Name] | $[Amount] | [X]% | [X]% |

2.2 **Additional Contributions:** No Member shall be required to make additional capital contributions without unanimous consent.

2.3 **Capital Accounts:** The Company shall maintain a capital account for each Member in accordance with applicable tax regulations.

2.4 **No Interest on Capital:** No Member shall receive interest on their capital contributions.

---

### Article III: Distributions & Allocations

3.1 **Profit/Loss Allocation:** Profits and losses shall be allocated to Members in proportion to their Membership Interests.

3.2 **Distributions:** Distributions shall be made [at such times and in such amounts as determined by the Members / quarterly / as specified]:

- Distributions shall be in proportion to Membership Interests
- Tax distributions shall be made in amounts sufficient to cover Members' tax obligations arising from Company income
- No distribution shall be made that would render the Company unable to pay its obligations

3.3 **Distribution Priority:**
1. Tax distributions
2. Return of capital contributions
3. Remaining profits per Membership Interest percentages

---

### Article IV: Management

4.1 **Management Structure:** The Company shall be [MEMBER-MANAGED / MANAGER-MANAGED].

**If Member-Managed:**
All Members shall participate in the management of the Company. Decisions shall be made by [majority vote / unanimous consent / specify].

**If Manager-Managed:**
The following Manager(s) shall manage the Company:

| Manager | Title | Authority |
|---|---|---|
| [Name] | [Managing Member / CEO] | [Scope of authority] |

4.2 **Major Decisions Requiring [Unanimous / Supermajority] Consent:**

- [ ] Admitting new Members
- [ ] Sale of substantially all assets
- [ ] Merging with another entity
- [ ] Taking on debt exceeding $[X]
- [ ] Entering contracts exceeding $[X]
- [ ] Changing the nature of the business
- [ ] Amending this Agreement
- [ ] Dissolving the Company

4.3 **Meetings:**
- [Frequency] meetings of Members
- [X] days written notice required
- [Quorum requirements]
- Actions may be taken without a meeting by written consent of [majority/all] Members

---

### Article V: Transfer of Interests

5.1 **Restrictions:** No Member may sell, transfer, assign, or encumber their Membership Interest without [the consent of all other Members / majority consent].

5.2 **Right of First Refusal:** Before transferring to a third party, the selling Member must first offer the interest to existing Members at the same price and terms.

5.3 **Permitted Transfers:** Transfers to [trusts for the benefit of the Member's family / wholly-owned entities] are permitted without consent.

5.4 **Valuation Method:** For any transfer, the Company's value shall be determined by [agreed-upon method: book value, appraisal, formula, etc.].

---

### Article VI: Withdrawal & Dissolution

6.1 **Voluntary Withdrawal:** A Member may withdraw with [X] days written notice. The withdrawing Member shall receive the fair value of their interest over [X] months.

6.2 **Events of Dissolution:**
- Written consent of [all / majority of] Members
- Entry of a judicial decree of dissolution
- Any event making it unlawful for the Company to continue

6.3 **Winding Up:** Upon dissolution, the Company shall wind up its affairs, liquidate assets, and distribute proceeds in the following order:
1. Payment of debts and obligations to creditors
2. Setting up reserves for contingent liabilities
3. Return of capital contributions to Members
4. Distribution of remaining assets per Membership Interests

---

### Article VII: Books, Records & Tax

7.1 **Books and Records:** The Company shall maintain complete and accurate books at its principal office.

7.2 **Fiscal Year:** The fiscal year shall be the calendar year.

7.3 **Tax Classification:** The Company shall be classified as [a partnership / disregarded entity / S-Corporation] for federal income tax purposes.

7.4 **Tax Matters Partner:** [Name] shall serve as the "Partnership Representative" for tax purposes.

7.5 **Tax Returns:** The Company shall prepare and file all required tax returns and provide K-1s to Members by [March 15 / within 60 days of fiscal year end].

---

### Article VIII: Indemnification

The Company shall indemnify and hold harmless each Member and Manager from any claims, losses, or liabilities arising from actions taken in good faith on behalf of the Company.

---

### Article IX: Dispute Resolution

9.1 **Mediation:** Disputes shall first be submitted to mediation.

9.2 **Arbitration:** If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in [CITY, STATE] under [AAA / JAMS] rules.

9.3 **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [STATE].

---

### Article X: General Provisions

10.1 **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement among the Members.

10.2 **Amendment:** This Agreement may be amended only by written consent of [all / majority of] Members.

10.3 **Severability:** If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall continue in effect.
