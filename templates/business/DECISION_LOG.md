> Record of significant business decisions. Captures context, options considered, rationale, and outcomes. Prevents re-litigating decisions and provides institutional memory.

---

## When to Log a Decision

Log a decision when ANY of these apply:
- It affects strategy, budget, or team structure
- It is difficult or costly to reverse
- Multiple stakeholders were involved or affected
- The rationale is non-obvious and might be questioned later
- It represents a change from previous practice
