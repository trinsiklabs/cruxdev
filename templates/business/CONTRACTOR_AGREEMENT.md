## INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into as of [DATE] ("Effective Date") by and between:

**Company:** [COMPANY NAME], a [state] [entity type], at [ADDRESS] ("Company")

**Contractor:** [CONTRACTOR NAME], [individual / entity type], at [ADDRESS] ("Contractor")

---

### 1. Services

Contractor agrees to perform the following services ("Services"):

[Detailed description of work to be performed, deliverables, and specifications.]

**Project/Engagement Name:** [Name]
**Start Date:** [Date]
**End Date:** [Date or "Ongoing until terminated"]

---

### 2. Compensation

| Compensation Type | Rate/Amount | Payment Terms |
|---|---|---|
| [Hourly / Fixed / Milestone] | $[X] per [hour/project/milestone] | [Net 15 / Net 30 / Upon completion] |

- **Invoicing:** Contractor shall submit invoices [frequency] to [email/system]
- **Expenses:** [Reimbursable expenses require prior approval / No expenses reimbursed]
- **Late payment:** Payments not received within [X] days of due date may accrue interest at [X]% per month

---

### 3. Relationship of Parties

**Contractor is NOT an employee of Company.** Contractor:

- Controls the manner, method, and means of performing the Services
- Is responsible for their own taxes (federal, state, self-employment)
- Is not entitled to employee benefits (health insurance, retirement, PTO, workers' comp)
- May work for other clients concurrently
- Provides their own equipment and workspace (unless otherwise agreed)

Company will issue a 1099-NEC (or equivalent) for payments exceeding $600 in a calendar year.

---

### 4. Intellectual Property

**Work Product Assignment:** All work product, inventions, designs, code, content, and other materials created by Contractor in performing the Services ("Work Product") shall be the sole and exclusive property of Company.

Contractor hereby assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in the Work Product, including all intellectual property rights.

**Pre-Existing IP:** Contractor retains ownership of any pre-existing intellectual property ("Contractor IP"). If Contractor IP is incorporated into the Work Product, Contractor grants Company a perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free, worldwide license to use such Contractor IP as part of the Work Product.

**Moral Rights:** To the extent permitted by law, Contractor waives any moral rights in the Work Product.

---

### 5. Confidentiality

Contractor agrees to:
- Hold all Company confidential information in strict confidence
- Not disclose confidential information to any third party
- Use confidential information solely for performing the Services
- Return or destroy all confidential information upon termination

This obligation survives termination for [2/3] years.

[Reference separate NDA if applicable: See also NDA dated [DATE].]

---

### 6. Non-Solicitation

During the term and for [X] months after termination, Contractor shall not directly solicit Company's employees or customers with whom Contractor had material contact during the engagement.

---

### 7. Representations & Warranties

Contractor represents and warrants that:
- Contractor has the right and ability to enter into this Agreement
- The Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner
- The Work Product will be original and will not infringe any third party's rights
- Contractor will comply with all applicable laws

---

### 8. Indemnification

Contractor shall indemnify Company against any claims arising from:
- Contractor's breach of this Agreement
- Contractor's negligence or willful misconduct
- Any claim that the Work Product infringes third-party rights

---

### 9. Limitation of Liability

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Company's total liability shall not exceed the total compensation paid or payable under this Agreement.

---

### 10. Termination

**By either Party:** [X] days written notice
**For cause:** Immediately upon material breach, if not cured within [X] days of notice
**Effect:** Upon termination:
- Contractor delivers all completed and in-progress Work Product
- Company pays for Services rendered through termination date
- Confidentiality obligations survive

---

### 11. Dispute Resolution

- **Governing Law:** State of [STATE]
- **Dispute resolution:** [Mediation then arbitration / Courts of [jurisdiction]]

---

### 12. General Provisions

- **Entire Agreement:** Supersedes all prior agreements on the subject
- **Amendment:** Written amendment signed by both Parties required
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect the remainder
- **Assignment:** Contractor may not assign without written consent
- **Notices:** Written notices to addresses listed above
