## Risk Scoring

### Likelihood Scale

| Score | Label | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | Almost Certain | >90% probability in next 12 months |
| 4 | Likely | 60-90% probability |
| 3 | Possible | 30-60% probability |
| 2 | Unlikely | 10-30% probability |
| 1 | Rare | <10% probability |

### Impact Scale

| Score | Label | Financial Impact | Operational Impact | Reputational Impact |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 5 | Critical | >$[X] or >50% revenue | Business closure / complete halt | National media, permanent damage |
| 4 | Major | $[X]-$[X] | Major disruption, weeks to recover | Significant customer loss |
| 3 | Moderate | $[X]-$[X] | Moderate disruption, days to recover | Some customer complaints |
| 2 | Minor | $[X]-$[X] | Minor disruption, hours to recover | Internal awareness only |
| 1 | Negligible | <$[X] | No operational impact | No external awareness |

### Risk Rating = Likelihood x Impact

| Rating | Range | Action Required |
|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | 20-25 | Immediate mitigation, board/owner visibility |
| **High** | 12-19 | Mitigation plan within 30 days |
| **Medium** | 6-11 | Monitor and mitigate within quarter |
| **Low** | 1-5 | Accept or monitor |
