### 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Competing Business"** means any business, entity, or individual that provides {{COMPETING_PRODUCTS_SERVICES}} or any substantially similar products or services.

1.2 **"Restricted Territory"** means {{RESTRICTED_TERRITORY}}.

1.3 **"Restricted Period"** means the period during Individual's relationship with Company and for {{NON_COMPETE_PERIOD}} after termination for any reason.

1.4 **"Company Clients"** means any person or entity that: (a) was a customer of Company during the {{CLIENT_LOOKBACK_PERIOD}} preceding termination; or (b) was a prospective customer with whom Company had substantive discussions during that period.

1.5 **"Confidential Information"** has the meaning set forth in Section 4.

### 2. NON-COMPETE COVENANT

2.1 During the Restricted Period and within the Restricted Territory, Individual shall not, directly or indirectly:

(a) Own, manage, operate, control, or participate in any Competing Business;

(b) Serve as an officer, director, employee, consultant, advisor, or agent of any Competing Business;

(c) Have any financial interest in any Competing Business, except as a passive holder of not more than {{PASSIVE_INVESTMENT_THRESHOLD: 2}}% of the outstanding securities of a publicly traded company.

2.2 **Scope Limitation.** This non-compete applies only to activities that are competitive with the specific business areas in which Individual was involved or had access to Confidential Information during the {{INVOLVEMENT_LOOKBACK}} preceding termination.

### 3. NON-SOLICITATION COVENANTS

3.1 **Customer Non-Solicitation.** During the Restricted Period, Individual shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit, contact, or attempt to divert any Company Client for the purpose of providing products or services competitive with Company's offerings.

3.2 **Employee/Contractor Non-Solicitation.** During the Restricted Period, Individual shall not, directly or indirectly, recruit, solicit, or induce any employee or contractor of Company to leave Company's service or to breach any agreement with Company.

3.3 **Clarification.** General advertising or job postings not specifically targeted at Company personnel do not constitute solicitation under Section 3.2.

### 4. CONFIDENTIALITY

4.1 **Confidential Information** means all non-public information relating to Company's business, including customer lists, pricing, business strategies, financial data, technical information, trade secrets, and any other proprietary information.

4.2 Individual shall not use or disclose Confidential Information during or after the relationship with Company, except as required in the performance of duties for Company.

4.3 This confidentiality obligation survives termination indefinitely with respect to trade secrets and for {{CONFIDENTIALITY_PERIOD}} with respect to other Confidential Information.

### 5. NON-DISPARAGEMENT

5.1 Individual shall not make any public statements that are disparaging or defamatory to Company, its products, services, officers, directors, or employees.

5.2 Company shall instruct its officers and directors not to make public statements that are disparaging or defamatory to Individual.

5.3 This provision does not restrict truthful statements made in legal proceedings, to government agencies, or as otherwise required by law.

### 6. REASONABLENESS

6.1 Individual acknowledges that: (a) the restrictions in this Agreement are reasonable and necessary to protect Company's legitimate business interests; (b) Company would not have entered into this arrangement without these protections; (c) Individual's skills and experience are sufficient to pursue alternative employment opportunities outside the restrictions.

6.2 If any restriction is deemed unreasonable by a court of competent jurisdiction, the Parties request that the court modify the restriction to the minimum extent necessary to make it enforceable rather than voiding it entirely.

### 7. CONSIDERATION

7.1 In exchange for Individual's covenants, Company provides: {{SPECIFIC_CONSIDERATION}}.

7.2 Individual acknowledges that the consideration is adequate and sufficient.

### 8. REMEDIES

8.1 Individual acknowledges that a breach would cause irreparable harm to Company for which monetary damages would be inadequate.

8.2 Company shall be entitled to injunctive relief and specific performance, in addition to any other remedies available at law or equity, without the requirement of posting a bond.

8.3 If Individual breaches any covenant, the Restricted Period shall be extended by the duration of the breach.

8.4 The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees.

### 9. SEVERABILITY AND MODIFICATION

9.1 Each covenant in this Agreement is independent and severable.

9.2 If any provision is found unenforceable, the court shall reform it to the minimum extent necessary to make it enforceable, and remaining provisions shall continue in full force.
