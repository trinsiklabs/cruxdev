## Retention Strategy

Growth means nothing if subscribers leave. Retention tactics:

1. **Consistent quality** — never send a filler issue
2. **Set expectations** — welcome email states frequency and content
3. **Engagement hooks** — ask for replies, run polls, feature subscriber questions
4. **Re-engagement** — automated sequence for inactive subscribers before removal
5. **Feedback loops** — quarterly survey asking what they want more/less of
