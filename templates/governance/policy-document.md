## Exceptions Process

1. **Request:** Officer submits exception request to policy owner with justification
2. **Review:** Policy owner evaluates risk and alternatives
3. **Approval:** Stream approves exceptions; Trio approves for security/compliance policies
4. **Documentation:** Exception recorded below with expiry date
5. **Expiry:** Exceptions automatically expire; renewal requires fresh approval
