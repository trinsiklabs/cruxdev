## Accountability Structure

**Principle: Delegation transfers operational authority, NEVER accountability.**

| Aspect | Delegator | Delegate |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Decision-making** | Retains veto right | Makes day-to-day decisions within scope |
| **Accountability** | Answers to Trio for outcomes | Reports to delegator |
| **Escalation** | Receives escalations from delegate | Escalates decisions outside scope |
| **Audit** | Subject to audit on delegation outcomes | Subject to audit on operational execution |

## Escalation Path

_When does the delegate escalate to the delegator?_

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Decision outside delegated scope | Escalate to delegator immediately |
| Conflict with another officer's authority | Escalate to Stream |
| Emergency requiring immediate action | Act, then notify delegator within 1h |
| Uncertainty about scope boundary | Escalate to delegator before acting |

| Review Type | Frequency | Reviewer | Purpose |

| Performance review | Per officer evaluation cycle | Stream | Evaluate delegate's exercise of authority |
| Renewal review | Before expiry date | Trio | Decide whether to renew, modify, or revoke |
