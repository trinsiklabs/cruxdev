## Title Optimization

**Rules:**
1. Under 60 characters (truncates on mobile at ~50)
2. Primary keyword within first 40 characters
3. Front-load the value proposition
4. Use numbers when applicable ("5 ways...", "in 10 minutes")
5. Test 3 title options before publishing (see video script template)

**Title patterns that work for this channel:**
- "How to [action] [benefit] in [timeframe]"
- "[Number] [topic] mistakes [audience] make"
- "[Topic]: [Specific technique] explained"
- "[Result] with [method] — [qualifier]"

## Description Optimization

**Structure:**
1. Lines 1-2: Hook + primary link (visible without "show more")
2. Lines 3-5: Summary with keywords
3. Timestamps
4. Links to mentioned resources
5. Social links
6. Hashtags (3-5)

**Keyword placement:** Natural usage in first 2 lines and summary. Do not keyword-stuff.

## Tags

- Use 5-15 tags per video
- First tag = exact primary keyword
- Mix broad and specific tags
- Include channel name as a tag (for suggested videos)
- Include common misspellings of technical terms

## Cards and End Screens

- **Card at 0:30-1:00:** Link to relevant playlist or video (capitalize on early engagement)
- **Card at halfway:** Link to related deep-dive video
- **End screen (last 20s):** Subscribe button + best related video + playlist
