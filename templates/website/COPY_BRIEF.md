## 4. Copy Constraints

- [ ] No jargon without definition
- [ ] No superlatives without proof (avoid "best", "fastest", "#1" unless substantiated)
- [ ] Active voice preferred over passive
- [ ] Second person ("you") for addressing the reader
- [ ] Consistent terminology — use the same word for the same concept across all pages
