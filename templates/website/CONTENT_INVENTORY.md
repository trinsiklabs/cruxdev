**Quality Score Key:**
- 5 = Excellent — current, accurate, well-written, high-performing
- 4 = Good — mostly current, minor updates needed
- 3 = Adequate — functional but needs significant improvement
- 2 = Poor — outdated, inaccurate, or underperforming
- 1 = Remove — no longer relevant or actively harmful

**Action Key:**
- **Keep** — No changes needed
- **Update** — Refresh content, keep URL
- **Merge** — Combine with another page
- **Redirect** — Remove page, redirect URL to better destination
- **Remove** — Delete (with redirect to relevant page or 410)

## 3. Content Quality Assessment

### 3.1 Assessment Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | How Assessed |
|---|---|---|
| Accuracy | High | Is the content factually correct and current? |
| Relevance | High | Does it serve the target audience's needs? |
| SEO optimization | Medium | Title tags, meta descriptions, headings, keyword usage |
| Readability | Medium | Clear language, logical structure, appropriate length |
| Visual quality | Medium | Images, formatting, whitespace, mobile rendering |
| Conversion alignment | High | Does it guide toward a desired action? |
| Freshness | Medium | When was it last updated? Is it dated? |
