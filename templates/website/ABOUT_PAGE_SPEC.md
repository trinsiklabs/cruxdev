# About Page Specification

> The about page builds trust and credibility. Visitors who click "About" are evaluating whether to trust you.

## 1. Page Mission

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Primary job | [Build credibility and human connection] |
| Target visitors | [Investors, partners, potential customers doing due diligence, job seekers] |
| Primary CTA | [e.g., "Contact Us" or "Join Our Team"] |
| Success metric | [e.g., "Low bounce rate, high time-on-page, clicks to Contact or Careers"] |

---

## 2. Content Blocks

### Block 1: Hero / Mission Statement

| Element | Content |
|---|---|
| Headline (H1) | [e.g., "We're building the future of [domain]"] |
| Mission statement | [2-3 sentences: What you do, why it matters, who benefits] |
| Visual | [Team photo, office, or mission-aligned imagery] |

### Block 2: Story / Origin

| Element | Content |
|---|---|
| Heading (H2) | [e.g., "How We Started" or "Our Story"] |
| Narrative | [Brief founding story — authentic, specific. Why this problem? Why this team? Key milestones.] |
| Timeline (optional) | [Key dates: Founded, first customer, funding, product launches] |

### Block 3: Values / Principles

| Element | Content |
|---|---|
| Heading (H2) | [e.g., "What We Believe"] |
| Value 1 | [Name + 1-2 sentence description] |
| Value 2 | [Name + 1-2 sentence description] |
| Value 3 | [Name + 1-2 sentence description] |

### Block 4: Team

| Element | Content |
|---|---|
| Heading (H2) | [e.g., "The Team" or "Meet the Founders"] |
| Layout | [Grid of headshots + name + title + brief bio] |
| Team member 1 | [Photo, name, title, 1-2 sentence bio, optional social links] |
| Team member 2 | [Same] |
| ... | [All team members or key team members] |

### Block 5: Traction / Credibility

| Element | Content |
|---|---|
| Metrics bar | [e.g., "500+ customers, $XM raised, Team of Y, Z countries"] |
| Press mentions | [Logo bar: publications that have covered you] |
| Awards | [Any relevant awards or recognitions] |
| Investors (optional) | [Investor logos if they add credibility] |

### Block 6: CTA

| Element | Content |
|---|---|
| Heading | [e.g., "Want to work with us?" or "Get in touch"] |
| CTA options | [Contact Us button, Careers link, or both] |
