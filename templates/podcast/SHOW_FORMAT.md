## Host Guidelines

- Introduce the topic before the guest (listener needs context)
- Ask one question at a time (compound questions confuse guests)
- Let silence happen (guests often fill silence with better answers)
- Redirect gently if the guest goes off-topic
- Always mention where to find the guest at the end
