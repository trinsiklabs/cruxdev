## Per-Episode Distribution Checklist

After each episode goes live:

- [ ] Verify episode appears on all platforms (check within 24h)
- [ ] Share on social media (audio clip + link)
- [ ] Include in next newsletter
- [ ] Create audiogram clips (2-3 per episode, 30-60 seconds each)
- [ ] Post show notes on website
- [ ] Cross-promote with guest (send them assets to share)
- [ ] Submit to any relevant podcast directories or aggregators
- [ ] Update episode page with transcript (for SEO)
