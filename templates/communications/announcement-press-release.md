## PRESS RELEASE GUIDELINES (Internal)

### Structure
1. **Headline:** Clear, newsworthy, under 100 characters. Verb-forward.
2. **Subheadline:** One sentence elaborating. Optional.
3. **Dateline:** City, State — Date
4. **Lead paragraph:** Who, what, when, where, why in the first paragraph.
5. **Body:** Supporting details, context, impact.
6. **Quotes:** One from company executive, one from partner/customer (if applicable).
7. **Boilerplate:** Standard company description.
8. **Contact:** Media relations contact.

### Writing Standards
- Write in third person
- Use active voice
- Avoid jargon and marketing superlatives ("revolutionary," "groundbreaking")
- Include specific numbers and facts
- Keep under 500 words
- One page maximum
