### 7. RESPONSE PRINCIPLES

1. **Speed.** Acknowledge quickly, even before you have all the facts. "We are aware and investigating" is better than silence.
2. **Transparency.** Share what you know, what you don't know, and what you're doing about it. Do not speculate.
3. **Empathy.** Acknowledge the impact on affected people. Lead with concern, not defensiveness.
4. **Accuracy.** Only share confirmed facts. Corrections erode trust more than initial uncertainty.
5. **Consistency.** One message, one voice. All spokespeople use the same approved language.
6. **Legal alignment.** All external statements reviewed by legal before release. But do not let legal review delay initial acknowledgment.

### 8. POST-CRISIS

Within {{POST_CRISIS_WINDOW}} after the crisis is resolved:

1. **Post-mortem:** Document what happened, timeline, decisions made, what worked, what didn't
2. **Final communication:** Inform all stakeholders of resolution and preventive measures
3. **Plan update:** Update this crisis plan based on lessons learned
4. **Follow-through:** Execute all commitments made during the crisis
5. **Reputation monitoring:** Track media and social sentiment for {{MONITORING_PERIOD}}
