### PRESENTATION GUIDELINES

| Guideline | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Length** | 12-15 slides, no more. Appendix for depth. |
| **Time** | {{DURATION}} minutes presenting, remainder for Q&A |
| **Slide density** | One key message per slide. Minimal text. |
| **Visuals** | Use charts, product shots, and customer logos. Avoid clip art. |
| **Font size** | Minimum 24pt. If you can't read it from the back of the room, it's too small. |
| **Narrative** | Tell a story: Problem > Solution > Proof > Opportunity > Team > Ask |
| **Practice** | Rehearse 3+ times. Time yourself. Prepare for common questions. |
